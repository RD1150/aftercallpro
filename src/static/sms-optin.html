How SMS Opt-In Works

AfterCallPro collects SMS consent via a web form (Web Form opt-in type) during account registration. Below is the complete opt-in workflow.

1
Business owner visits aftercallpro.com/signup and fills in their business name, email, phone number, and password.
2
The SMS consent checkbox is displayed unchecked with the full consent language. The user must actively check the box — it is never pre-selected.
3
The user checks the box, confirming they agree to receive SMS notifications from AfterCallPro. The "Create Free Account" button becomes active only after the box is checked.
4
A confirmation SMS is sent to the phone number provided: "You have been opted in to receive SMS notifications from AfterCallPro. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg & data rates may apply."
5
The user can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. They will receive a confirmation: "You have been unsubscribed from AfterCallPro SMS. No further messages will be sent."
