Privacy Policy

AfterCallPro - 24/7 AI Call Assistant

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AfterCallPro collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered call answering service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account with AfterCallPro, we collect:

  • Business name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

Call Data

To provide our AI call answering service, we collect and process:

  • Incoming caller phone numbers
  • Call duration and timestamps
  • Call recordings and transcripts
  • AI-generated call summaries
  • Sentiment analysis results

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our service:

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time and date of access

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Answer calls on your behalf using AI, generate transcripts, and provide analytics
  • Improve Quality: Train and improve our AI models for better call handling
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Communications: Send service updates, billing information, and important notices
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve our service

3. Third-Party Services

AfterCallPro integrates with the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

Twilio

We use Twilio for telephony services to receive and manage phone calls. Twilio's privacy policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

OpenAI

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 to power our AI call assistant. Call transcripts are processed by OpenAI to generate intelligent responses. OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Render.com

Our application is hosted on Render.com infrastructure. Render's privacy policy: https://render.com/privacy

Important: We do not sell your personal information or call data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review our security practices
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted in secure, monitored facilities

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Call Records: Retained for 90 days by default (configurable in settings)
  • Billing Information: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account closure

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your call history and transcripts
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aftercallpro.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

AfterCallPro is a business service and is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International Users

AfterCallPro is operated from the United States. If you are accessing our service from outside the US, please be aware that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to your registered email address

Your continued use of AfterCallPro after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
