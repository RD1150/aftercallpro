Step 2 — What Happens After Opt-In

Once a business owner opts in and creates their account, AfterCallPro monitors their business phone line. When a customer calls and cannot reach the business, AfterCallPro sends a single automated SMS follow-up to that customer on behalf of the business.

1

Consumer calls the business

A customer dials the business phone number. The call goes unanswered or to voicemail.

2

AfterCallPro AI answers

AfterCallPro's AI assistant engages the caller and captures their inquiry.

3

Single SMS follow-up is sent

One transactional SMS is sent to the caller's number as a direct response to their inquiry.

Hi, this is [Business Name]. We missed your call. Please reply with your request or call us back. Reply STOP to opt out.
4

Consumer can opt out at any time

Replying STOP immediately removes the consumer from all future messages.

You have been unsubscribed. You will not receive any more messages from this number. Reply START to resubscribe.
